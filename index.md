# Privacy Policy for Captain's Haul

**Last Updated:** 05 Jan 2026

## Introduction

This privacy policy explains how Captain's Haul ("the Game") collects, uses, and protects your information when you play our game.

## Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Data
- **Anonymous Player ID**: A unique identifier created by PlayFab to track your game progress and scores
- **Game Statistics**: Your scores, game mode preferences (Normal/Hardcore), and gameplay data
- **Device Information**: Basic device type and platform (Android/iOS) for technical support

### Data You Provide
- **Player Display Name** (optional): If you choose to customize your display name in settings
- **Purchase Information**: Transaction records if you make in-app purchases

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Save your game progress and scores
- Display leaderboards and rankings
- Process in-app purchases
- Improve game performance and fix bugs
- Ensure fair play and prevent cheating

## Third-Party Services

Captain's Haul uses the following third-party services:

### PlayFab (Microsoft)
- **Purpose**: Game backend, leaderboards, player authentication
- **Data Shared**: Anonymous player ID, scores, gameplay statistics
- **Privacy Policy**: https://privacy.microsoft.com/privacystatement

### Unity Services
- **Purpose**: Game engine and analytics
- **Data Shared**: Basic device info, app performance data
- **Privacy Policy**: https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy

## Data Storage and Security
- All player data is stored securely on PlayFab's servers
- We use industry-standard encryption and security measures
- Your data is stored anonymously - we do not collect personal information like email addresses or real names

## Your Rights
You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request a copy of your data
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your game data
- **Opt-Out**: Stop playing the game at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at: lillilgames234@gmail.com

## Children's Privacy

Captain's Haul does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The game uses anonymous identifiers only and does not require account creation with personal details.

## International Users

Your data may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside your country. By playing Captain's Haul, you consent to this transfer.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date will reflect the most recent changes. Continued use of the game after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
